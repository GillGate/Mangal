Welcome to Mangal restaurant

“Mangal” restaurant was opened in 2015 right in the heart of Prague - on the top of Wenceles square. During this time the restaurant has earned the trust of the residents and guests of Czech Republic.

Turkish cuisine is startling in its variety and plenty of dishes, which absolutely everyone will like. Dish preparation meets the best Turkish samples, which is confirmed by the professionalism of the chefs from Turkey.

The specialty of the restaurant is а grill right in the restaurant, that allows guests to watch the process of cooking their dish. The restaurant offers a wide choice of lamb, chicken and veal that cooked on natural coals, and fresh made various meze-starters.

Guests can also enjoy dishes from the oven such as pide which is boat-shaped bread topped with meat or cheese, and It is impossible not to mention the traditional Turkish desserts. Special attention should be paid to the national drinks such as Ayran and Shalgam.

Only halal meat is used in the preparation of all dishes. Restaurant hall is decorated with all the features of traditional Turkish restaurants.
Come and enjoy little Turkey in the heart of Prague!
